##How to motivate a company to apply analytics?
## Required Data -------------------------- - Recency - What was the last time an action was taken? - Frequency - How many times the action was taken in a specific period of time?
## Applications -------------------------- - Predicting how often a visitor will return to your website. (Alive = visiting. Die = decided the website wasn't for them) - Understanding how frequently a patient may return to a hospital. (Alive = visiting. Die = maybe the patient moved to a new city, or became deceased.) - Predicting individuals who have churned from an app using only their usage history. (Alive = logins. Die = removed the app) - Predicting repeat purchases from a customer. (Alive = actively purchasing. Die = became disinterested with your product) - Predicting the lifetime values of your customers

## Assumptions -------------------------- - The phenomenon being modelled is a sequence of independent trials. - There are only two possible outcomes for each trial, often designated success or failure. - The probability of success, p, is the same for every trial.

## Implementation in Python -------------------------- 

```
from lifetimes import BetaGeoFitter
bgf = BetaGeoFitter()
bgf.fit(data['frequency'], data['recency'], data['T'])
from lifetimes.plotting import plot_frequency_recency_matrix
plot_frequency_recency_matrix(bgf)
from lifetimes.plotting import plot_probability_alive_matrix
plot_probability_alive_matrix(bgf)
```
